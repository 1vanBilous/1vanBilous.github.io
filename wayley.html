Wayley

(October 2017 – January 2019)
In the first year of my undergraduate degree I met a like-minded person who soon became my friend. Together we founded the startup that was aimed at helping Kharkiv citizens and tourists of this city. It was a mobile application with interesting walking routes, city's attractions, and venues. Application was in development from October 2017 to January 2019 and was removed from Google Play in February 2019.
