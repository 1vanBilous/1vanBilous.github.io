Genetic Algorithm Solves a Maze

(September 2020 - October 2020)
Studying evolutionary algorithms at my university I decided to take the initiative and explore this topic in more detail. I developed a tool for building mazes and then implemented a genetic algorithm for solving these mazes with visualization of the process using Unity engine. Additionally, my application allows users to change the parameters of the algorithm and observe how this affects its efficiency.
